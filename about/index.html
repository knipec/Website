About Me


Hi, I'm Carissa. I make stuff that other people call art. Reasons I do this include:
  • It's fun
  • Sometimes I think about things
  • Sometimes I want other people to think about those things
Things I sometimes think about include:
  • Human nature, societies, and identity
  • How we judge artistic value
  • Nature + humans
  • Seeing things we take for granted in a new way
  • Obscure confusing metaphor
Other facts:
  • I studied studio art and computer science at Carleton College in Northfield, MN
  • I'm currently in Seattle, WA
