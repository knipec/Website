About Me


Hi, I'm Carissa. I'm currently in Seattle, WA, and I make stuff that people call art. Sometimes my arts practice is around any or none of the following:

  • Art I make is a little part of myself that I can share with others (and/or is for myself)
  • Trying to dream of a world of radical compassion and acceptance, where we are growing and healing and imagining and building and being and maybe it's beautiful
  • Plants! They're so cool!
  • Things! They're kinda funny, or sad, or interesting sometimes!
  • Making art can be fun, an enjoyable challenge, and/or relaxing, and I think we deserve all that in this life
